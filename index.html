Why Choose Us

• Free Quotes

We believe in transparency and making it easy for our clients to plan with confidence. That’s why TSGlass and Aluminium offers free, no obligation quotations for all projects, big or small. Whether you're still exploring options or ready to get started, our team will assess your requirements and provide a clear, detailed quote to help you make an informed decision. No hidden fees. No pressure. Just honest guidance and pricing you can trust.

• Quality Materials

At TSGlass and Aluminium, we use only the finest quality materials sourced from trusted suppliers. All our products are made using SABS and AAAMSA approved materials, ensuring they meet the highest safety and performance standards. Our aluminium frames are designed for strength, durability, and weather resistance, while our glass solutions are both stylish and secure. This commitment to quality guarantees that every installation not only looks exceptional but also delivers long-lasting value with minimal maintenance.

• Experienced Team

Our team brings years of hands-on experience in the glass and aluminium industry, delivering excellence on every project. From design to installation, we are skilled, knowledgeable, and committed to getting the job done right the first time. Whether it’s a small residential upgrade or a large commercial fit-out, you can trust our team to provide expert advice, precision workmanship, and dependable service every step of the way.

• Free At-Home Measurements

Getting the perfect fit starts with accurate measurements. That’s why we offer free at-home site visits to take precise measurements before any installation begins. Our team will come to your location at a convenient time, assess the space, and guide you on the best product options for your needs. This ensures a seamless installation process and eliminates costly errors. All at no extra cost to you.

• Fast Turnaround

We understand that time is valuable, which is why we prioritise efficiency without compromising on quality. At TSGlass and Aluminium, we work with well-structured timelines to ensure that your project is completed promptly and professionally. From the initial quote to final installation, our team is focused on delivering results quickly and reliably, so you can enjoy your upgraded space sooner.

• Customer Satisfaction

At TSGlass and Aluminium, your satisfaction is our top priority. We go above and beyond to ensure every client receives personalised service, clear communication, and exceptional results. From your first enquiry to the final installation, we aim to make the entire process smooth, stress-free, and rewarding. Our growing base of happy clients is a reflection of our commitment to quality service and lasting relationships.

• Affordable Pricing

We believe that quality should never be out of reach. At TSGlass and Aluminium, we offer competitive and transparent pricing to suit a wide range of budgets without compromising on materials or workmanship. Our goal is to provide high-quality glass and aluminium solutions that offer real value for money. With us, you get premium results at a price that makes sense.

• Fully Registered & Compliant (AAAMSA & SAGGA)

TSGlass and Aluminium operates with full compliance to South African industry standards. We are proudly registered with AAAMSA (Association of Architectural Aluminium Manufacturers of South Africa) and SAGGA (South African Glass and Glazing Association). These affiliations reflect our commitment to quality, safety, and professionalism in every project we undertake. When you work with us, you’re choosing a company that meets recognised standards and delivers with integrity.
